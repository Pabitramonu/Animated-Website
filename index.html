The Avengers is a popular fictional superhero team that appears in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its first appearance in "The Avengers" released in 1963, created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby.The Avengers typically consist of a group of diverse and iconic Marvel superheroes, including characters like Iron Man (Tony Stark), Captain America (Steve Rogers), Thor, Hulk (Bruce Banner), Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff), and Hawkeye (Clint Barton).
